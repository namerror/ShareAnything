What services does the website provide?

The website mainly serves as a collection of a wide range of other websites and products - a map/dictionary in the computer world. Our aim is to provide support for individuals that are looking for external tools in their academic life / career. We also recommend plenty of tools useful in every aspect possible: whether you are looking for help in college application, or you are just interested in filmmaking, we have something for you. Recently we are considering an expansion: the new feature 'Share you thought' is coming out, which is a place where you can share any thoughts anonymously.
